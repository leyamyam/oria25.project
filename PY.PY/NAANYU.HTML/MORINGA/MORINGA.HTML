What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to think and act like humans. It includes learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

Types of AI

  • Narrow AI: Performs specific tasks, like chatbots or voice assistants.
  • General AI: Can perform any intellectual task like a human.
  • Super AI: Exceeds human intelligence and capabilities (still theoretical).

Why Learn AI?

AI is transforming every industry. Learning AI builds skills for the future, promotes innovation, and improves decision-making and problem-solving abilities.

Benefits of AI in Samburu

  • Smarter healthcare and education systems
  • Enhanced agricultural productivity
  • Job creation in tech and innovation fields

Current Trends in AI

  • Use of AI chatbots in government services
  • AI-driven data analysis for planning
  • Machine learning in agriculture and healthcare
  • AI education programs for youth and public servants
